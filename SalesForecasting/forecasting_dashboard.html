Model Formulation & Diagnostic Details

  • Autocorrelation Check: Daily sales show close to zero autocorrelation (Lag 1 correlation is -0.0355), confirming the series behaves as a stationary, noisy series around a slightly declining trend.
  • Seating & Capacity Constraints: Visualized peak forecasted days correspond to Thursdays and Tuesdays, suggesting these days are operationally efficient.
  • Scaled Aggregated Error: Aggregated forecast standard errors scale as \sigma \times \sqrt{N}, giving tighter relative bounds over weekly/monthly horizons.
